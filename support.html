FAQ

How do I create a new deck?
Tap the “+” icon in the Decks tab, then add your cards.
Can I import shared decks?
Yes—go to Settings → Import Deck, and paste the deck link.
How do I backup my decks?
Enable Cloud Backup in Settings, then sign in with your email.
