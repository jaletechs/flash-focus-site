Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: Deck interactions, session durations, quiz results.
  • User-Generated Content: Your custom flashcards and deck data.
  • Device Info: OS version, device model, unique identifiers for troubleshooting.
  • Optional Email: If you sign up for newsletters or backup features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve the App: Analyze features usage to fix bugs and enhance functionality.
  • Personalization: Save your preferences and custom decks.
  • Communication: Send updates, newsletters, or respond to support inquiries.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics for general usage statistics without any personal data. Crash reports are collected via Firebase Crashlytics, ensuring no personal identifiers are shared.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Captures anonymous crash reports.
  • Plausible Analytics: Tracks overall usage without cookies.

5. Your Control & Rights

  • Export or delete your decks anytime via the app settings.
  • Opt-out of analytics collection in the app’s Privacy settings.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

Questions or requests? Email us at support@jaletechs.com.
